Inheritance

## Properties

- **crop** (`undefined | false | true`) — Crop extra image if it's larger than the main chart.
- **marginBottom** (`undefined | number`) — Bottom margin in pixels.
- **marginLeft** (`undefined | number`) — Left margin in pixels.
- **marginRight** (`undefined | number`) — Right margin in pixels.
- **marginTop** (`undefined | number`) — Top margin in pixels.
- **position** (`"left" | "right" | "top" | "bottom"`) — Default "bottom" Position to place extra image in releation to the main chart.
